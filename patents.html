GPNE Corp. holds more than 30 patents worldwide. The validity of GPNE’s key patent has been reexamined by the U.S. Patent and Trademark Office. Research In Motion (makers of BlackBerry) initiated the reexamination request in October 2012. After an intensive review by the Reexamination Unit of the U.S. Patent and Trademark Office, the validity of GPNE’s patent was confirmed.
